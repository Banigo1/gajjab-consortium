Our Company

GAJJAB Consortium is a technology driven supply chain & logistic services company that provides safe, secure and excellent logistics services in West Africa. We provide full range of service in the sphere of customs clearance and transport for any type of cargo within Nigeria. We pride ourselves on providing the best transport and shipping services available in South West Nigeria.
We utilise the latest communications, tracking and processing web application for integrated supply chain solutions.
Established in January 2025, we consist of six co-founders based in Abuja, Niger, Port Harcourt and Lagos. The six co-founders are career experts in project management, procurement, supply chain and business commercials.
Company valuation (physical & cash assets) at commencement of Operations was 2000 USD.
